Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani

For my second homework assignment, I decided to make a short biography for Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani, the Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs for the State of Qatar. Sheikh Mohammed has been at the forefront of solving and mediating various conflicts and disputes, not just in the region but across the world. My goal is to showcase his background and expertise in foreign affairs.

Sheikh Mohammed was born on November 1, 1980, in Qatar's capital city, Doha. He earned his bachelor's degree in economics and business administration from the College of Business and Economics at Qatar University in 2003. After graduation, he served in various positions across multiple ministries and organizations in Qatar, such as the Ruling Family Council and the Economic Affairs Council, before officially joining the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in 2013. Below are his current positions, foreign honors, and some of his famous quotes while serving under the current Emir, Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani.

Ministerial Positions for the Government of Qatar

  1. Minister of Foreign Affairs: Appointed January 27, 2016
  2. Prime Minister of Qatar: Appointed March 7, 2023

Board/Member Positions in Qatari Companies and Organizations

  • Chairman of Qatar Fund for Development (QFFD)
  • Member of the Supreme Council for Economic Affairs and Investment

Foreign Honors

  • National Order of Merit (Paraguay)
  • Department of Defense Medal for Distinguished Public Service (United States)

Famous Quotes

  • "No country can dictate anything to the Palestinians."
  • "We want to restore the Middle East to its former greatness."
  • "We heard many promising things from the new administration in Syria. The road is still long to achieve stability in Syria."
