Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani

For my second homework assignment, I decided to make a short biography for Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani, the Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs for the State of Qatar. Sheikh Mohammed has been at the forefront of solving and mediating various different conflicts and disputes between countries not in the region but the whole world. What I hope to acheieve is to showcase his background and expertise in foreign affairs.

Sheikh Mohammed was born on November 1, 1980 in Qatar's capital city, Doha, and earned his bachelor's degree in conomics and business administration from the College of Business and Economics at Qatar University in 2003. After graduation, he served in various positions acorss multiple ministries and organizations for the State of Qatar, such as Ruling Family Council, Economic Affairs Council, and many more before officially joining the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in 2013. Listed below are his current positions and foreign honors:

  • Member of the Supreme Council for Economic Affairs and Investment
