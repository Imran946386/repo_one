National Colors of Qatar vs Saudi Arabia

Qatar's National Color

The national color of Qatar is an exotic maroon that is highlighted on its national flag. It originates from the purple dye industry that Qatar is known for throughout its history, as well as differentiating itself from the usual flag colors such as black and red usually seen throughout the region. Saudi Arabia's National Color

Saudi Arabia, on the other hand, uses a very rich green for its iconic flag color due to its association with Islam and the various empires that governed the Arabian Peninsula over the centuries.

Even though Saudi Arabia and Qatar use vastly different colors to contrast the white color on both of their flags, they both look equally beautiful in real life.
